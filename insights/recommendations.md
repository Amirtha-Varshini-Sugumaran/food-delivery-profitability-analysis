# Recommendations

## Priority 1: Reduce Delay Hotspots

Focus first on city-zone combinations with high delay rates and low ratings. Review driver coverage, promise times, and restaurant prep-time issues.

## Priority 2: Tighten Discount Strategy

Move away from broad high discounts. Use discounts for first repeat, win-back, or high-margin customer retention rather than blanket promotions.

## Priority 3: Protect High-Profit Customers

Create simple customer segments for high-profit loyal customers and high-profit occasional customers. Do not treat all repeat customers as equally valuable.

## Priority 4: Improve Restaurant Performance Reviews

Review restaurants with both strong demand and high operational risk. Share delay and rating feedback with restaurant partners.

## Priority 5: Use The Repeat Prediction Model Carefully

Use the model as a support tool, not an automated decision engine. Combine repeat likelihood with profit and service metrics.

# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Dashboard Goal

Help leadership, operations, and commercial teams see where growth is profitable, where delivery reliability is weak, and where retention activity is worth funding.

## Page 1: Executive Overview

- KPI cards: total orders, gross profit, average margin, repeat rate, delay rate, average rating
- Monthly trend: orders and gross profit
- City comparison: profit, delay rate, and repeat rate
- Alert table: zones with high delay rate and low rating

## Page 2: Profitability

- Profit by city and cuisine
- Margin by discount band
- Restaurant profit ranking with delay rate
- Scatter plot: order value vs gross profit
- Table of high-volume low-margin customer groups

## Page 3: Delivery Performance

- Delay rate by city and zone
- Average delivery time vs promised delivery time
- Weather impact on delays
- Restaurant-zone delay ranking
- Rating distribution for delayed vs non-delayed orders

## Page 4: Customer Retention

- Repeat rate by tenure group
- Repeat rate by discount band
- Profit per customer segment
- Delay experience by customer segment
- Matrix: acquisition channel by discount band and repeat rate

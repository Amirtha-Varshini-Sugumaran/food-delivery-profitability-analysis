# Chart Plan

| Chart | Used For | Why It Helps |
|---|---|---|
| KPI cards | Executive overview | Gives a quick read on volume, profit, delay, and retention |
| Line chart | Monthly order and profit trend | Shows whether growth and profit move together |
| Bar chart | Profit by city and cuisine | Easy comparison of commercial performance |
| Scatter plot | Order value vs gross profit | Highlights high-value but low-margin orders |
| Matrix | City-zone delay rate | Makes operational hotspots easy to spot |
| Box plot | Rating by delay flag | Shows service experience impact clearly |
| Stacked bar | Discount band by repeat flag | Shows whether discounts are linked to repeat behaviour |
| Ranked table | Restaurant-zone risk | Useful for operational action, not just reporting |

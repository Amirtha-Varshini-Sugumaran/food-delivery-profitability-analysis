# Food Delivery Profitability Dashboard

## Dashboard Snapshot

| KPI | Value | Business Read |
|---|---:|---|
| Total Orders | 3,200 | Strong operating sample across multiple cities |
| Gross Profit | INR 327,872 | Positive margin pool, but uneven by segment |
| Average Order Value | INR 454.76 | Healthy basket size for delivery analysis |
| Average Margin | 26.38% | Margin needs to be protected from broad discounts |
| Repeat Rate | 45.06% | Retention is meaningful but varies by customer group |
| Delay Rate | 76.62% | Delivery reliability is the main operational risk |
| Average Rating | 3.78 | Ratings are pulled down by delayed orders |
| Total Discount | INR 149,951.93 | Discount spend needs tighter targeting |

## Page 1: Executive Overview

Purpose: show the overall health of the business.

Core visuals:

- KPI cards for orders, gross profit, margin, repeat rate, delay rate, and rating
- Monthly order and profit trend
- City-level profit ranking
- High-risk zones table

Main question answered:

Are we growing profitably, or are discounts and delivery issues weakening the quality of growth?

## Page 2: Profitability

Purpose: show where the platform makes money and where margin is weak.

Core visuals:

- Gross profit by city
- Gross profit by cuisine type
- Margin by discount band
- Restaurant performance table with profit, delay rate, and rating

Main question answered:

Which cities, cuisines, and restaurant groups are commercially attractive after discounts and refunds?

## Page 3: Delivery Performance

Purpose: identify operational problems that affect customer experience.

Core visuals:

- Delay rate by city and zone
- Average delay minutes by zone
- Rating comparison for delayed vs on-time orders
- Weather and weekend delay patterns

Main question answered:

Where should operations act first to reduce late deliveries and protect ratings?

## Page 4: Customer Retention

Purpose: connect repeat behaviour with profit and service quality.

Core visuals:

- Repeat rate by tenure group
- Repeat rate by discount band
- Customer segment summary
- Acquisition channel by repeat rate and profit

Main question answered:

Which customers should the business retain with targeted offers, and which customers are expensive to keep?

## Dashboard Filters

## Dashboard Value

This dashboard helps recruiters and reviewers quickly see the project’s business focus: profitability, customer retention, delivery reliability, and practical decision-making.
